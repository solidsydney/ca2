Technical Support
At DHL we are committed to providing industry leading technical support for our customers.
Please call 1-855-345-7447 (option # 4) and a technical support analyst will be available to assist you with any of our electronic shipping systems.

Accounts Receivable
Our invoice provides the detailed shipping information you need to help track your weekly waybill transactions more efficiently. Plus, with our eBilling electronic invoicing system, you can view your invoices online and analyze your monthly shipping costs. We also offer a number of different payment options to help make the payment process faster and more convenient.

Please call 1-855-616-7344 and an accounts receivable analyst will be available to assist you with any of your questions. Our email address is customeraccounting.help@dhl.com

Unpaid Duty and Taxes Invoices
The DHL Accounts Receivable Department contacts its customers for unpaid Duty and Taxes invoices using phone number 1-855-380-9508. You should call this number only if a message has been left to return the call or contact with respect to unpaid duty and taxes charges.

Claims
If you have to submit a claim for damaged or lost items, our trained Claims Coordinators will strive to make the process as simple and trouble-free as possible.

Please call 1-855-345-7447 and one of our claims analysts will be available to assist you with any of your questions.
