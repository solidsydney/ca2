 DHL Canada |  Careers | Operations & Support

Join us and become part of a great team

You help us optimize our services – and we support you in advancing your professional development.

DHL satisfies its customers by offering a broad range of quality services. We satisfy our employees by providing an array of exciting career opportunities.

As a part of DHL, you play an important role in delivering quality services and solutions to our customers. We offer a diverse number of challenging opportunities in different functions and aspects of our business.

You could be working in different, exciting roles in our day-to-day operations and support services, whether in seaports or airports, warehouses or service centres, our offices or on the road.
As part of your professional development, we are committed to supporting you in improving your skills and expanding your expertise.

Through various on-site trainings as well as on mylearningworld.net, our online learning portal, we offer a wide range of opportunities for further education, training and development.

On-the-job training, which is important for you to gain skills, knowledge and certification to support you in responding better to the demands of your job, is also provided.
Some of the different training and development opportunities provided cover:
  • Different relevant operational knowledge & skills training
  • Health & safety training
  • Leadership training and courses
  • Language courses
  • Soft skills training
  • Basic management techniques

Improve with us!

Would you like to be a part of DHL? Then join our team! We provide opportunities for operational & support experts and specialists in many functional areas across the world.

Find the right opportunity for you by exploring our Jobs page or by taking part in one of our programs.
