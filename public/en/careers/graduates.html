Your chance to make a difference

Are you looking for a challenge that‚Äôs more than just a job? Welcome to DHL! We give you the chance to make a difference.

DHL provides graduates and career starters with various opportunities to join exciting projects, master demanding challenges and assume responsibility – the right way to launch your career.

Our graduate programs optimally prepare you for these challenges – and your future career.
On a global level, DHL offers different graduate programs:
The GROW Program offers participants the opportunity to obtain major responsibility at national and international locations, extensive project-related experience and targeted career guidance.
Read more about the GROW Program
The DHL Supply Chain Graduate Program offers the chance for graduates to join a dynamic global company with exceptional opportunities to experience different cultures and fast-track their way into operational roles. There are places on the Program in countries through Europe and across the Middle East and Africa regions.
Read more about the DHL Supply Chain Graduate Program
Together with AIESEC, the world’s largest student-run organization, we organize the international exchange of interns. Students and recent graduates from many countries around the world use the chance to gain on-the-job experience and put their skills to use in exciting projects.
Read more about AIESEC Internships
As part of your professional development, we offer you a broad range of further-education courses & programs that provide you with skills and knowledge necessary to ensure your success in your working environment. This includes training opportunities in various expert and operational knowledge & skills, leadership, soft skills and management techniques on mylearningworld.net, our online learning portal.
We have also implemented motiv8, our performance management system. It ensures that we recognize our employees’ potential early on. This enables us to support your growth for the future.

Grow With Us!

Whether you are a graduate looking to directly launch a career or one who already has some professional experience - Find the right opportunity for you by exploring our Jobs page or by taking part in one of our programs.
