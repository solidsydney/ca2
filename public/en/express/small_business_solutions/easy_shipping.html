Shipping with DHL – 5 Easy Steps

We make shipping with DHL easy for you. Whether you are new to DHL or a returning customer, these key steps to shipping help get your shipments moving to their destination.
Already know what service you want? Shipment prepared and ready to ship right now?
Ship Online Now
1 - Quote

Where, When and How Much
Tell us what you want to ship, where it’s going, when you need it to be there and get a quote.

Before you send your shipment, you will more than likely need to know what the cost will be. To calculate cost, we need to know the exact destination as well as the size and weight of your shipment.

2 - Prepare

Prepare Your Shipment
It is essential that the shipment and any relevant paperwork are ready for when your courier calls or when you drop off your package at a DHL SERVICE POINT location.
Whether you are packing the shipment yourself or you are using DHL branded packaging, please ensure that your shipment contents are adequately protected.

A waybill needs to be completed for every express shipment you send with DHL. And, if your package is declarable to customs, you will also need to prepare customs paperwork.

The good news is that if you ship online, you will be able to create the necessary paperwork as you go along.

You can also learn more about shipment paperwork and customs, as well as access an easy-to-use online customs paperwork tool.

5 - Pay

Choose One of Our Flexible Payment Options
DHL can offer you a range of flexible payment and billing options, putting you in control and saving your small business valuable administration time.

DHL non-account holders can pay for shipments by cash or credit card, while account holders can benefit from regular invoicing.
DHL account holders also have the convenience of managing invoices with DHL’s electronic billing service. This allows you to view new e-invoices, search through archived invoices and import invoice data directly into your accounting system.
