Go Paperless with Electronic Billing

Save time, gain efficiencies and receive your invoices via email with our electronic billing service.
Overview

What is electronic billing?

E-Billing is an electronic format of DHL Canada’s invoices. It is the fastest and easiest way to receive your invoices. Multiple formats are available for ultimate ease of use and flexibility.
  • Receive email notification of new invoices, including key details such as invoice amounts and due dates
  • Reduce your costs by eliminating the charges associated with receiving paper invoices
  • Access your invoices in multiple ways
  • Choose from five different options
  • View invoice and shipment details in an easy to follow format
  • Organize and rearrange the data easily
  • View your invoices from work, from home, from anywhere

What is DHL EzyBill?

EzyBill is an environmentally friendly solution and easy to use web-based tool that enables you to manage your DHL invoices real-time, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. With EzyBill, you will enjoy instant access to summary and detailed invoice data, including related shipment images such as waybills, commercial invoices and other customs documentation. It is a paperless, faster and more up-to-date way of keeping track of your financial transactions with DHL.
  • View and download copies of all invoices, including corresponding PDF and TIF documentation
  • Download invoice and shipment transaction data in spreadsheet format
  • Query and submit disputes in real time at the invoice level
  • Designate a Company Administrator to create new logins for multiple internal users
  • Access invoice and shipment documentation images for up to 12 months
  • You can view both the DHL Custom Duty Invoice and corresponding entry documentation
To get started with DHL EzyBill, please complete the online registration form to obtain a user ID and password.
