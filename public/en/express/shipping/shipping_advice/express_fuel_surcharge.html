Fuel Surcharge

Changes in fuel prices result in fluctuating costs for the transport industry, necessitating a variable fuel surcharge which may rise, fall or be removed, in line with movements in fuel prices.
MonthSurcharge
May 20151.5%
April 20152.5%
March 20150.5%
The fuel surcharge for international, Time Definite and Day Definite services will be based on the monthly average spot prices for kerosene/jet fuel (monthly average spot prices are found on the U.S Gulf Coast Index), as published by the US Department of Energy. Prices are expressed in (USD) cents per US gallon.
There will be a two month lag in the application of the index. For example, the monthly average price for fuel in January is used to determine the applicable surcharge for March. This is due to the release dates of the spot pricing data.

The fuel surcharge applies to transportation charges on all services and to the following surcharges (where applicable):
  • Non Standard Day (for Pickup or Delivery)
  • Remote Area Service
  • Non Standard Pickup
  • Repeat Delivery Attempt
  • Non Standard Delivery
  • Over Weight Piece
  • Over Sized Piece
DHL Express reserves the right to change the fuel surcharge index and table with or without notice. Both the amount and duration of the surcharge will be determined at DHL's sole discretion.
Customers with Fixed Rate Volume Discounts may be charged a different fuel surcharge from those indicated above. For more information on Fixed Rate fuel surcharges please contact your DHL Sales Representative.
