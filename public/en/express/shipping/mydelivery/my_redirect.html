DHL My Re-Direct ‚Äì Deliver to a Business Address

 


Have your shipment delivered to a business address instead of your residence.
 
Important
DHL’s My Re-Direct is only available within the same service area as the original delivery address. For example, shipments originally destined for an address in Ottawa cannot be re-directed to an address in Montreal. Please contact DHL for more information about specific service areas. Redirection will incur additional transit time to the times quoted originally. Requests received after 6pm EST will incur an additional transit day as they will not be processed until the following business day. You should provide DHL with a business address for your redelivery if available. Redelivery to most areas will take place within 2-3 business days, depending on the location of the alternative address. If two delivery attempts have already been made then any duties and taxes due MUST be paid in advance before a courier can be sent for a third delivery attempt.  Please Click here to pay your delivery duties, taxes and fees securely online.
