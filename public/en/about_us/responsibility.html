Corporate Responsibility

Corporate Responsibility is an integral part of our corporate strategy. With “Living Responsibility” as our motto, we focus on environmental protection (GoGreen), disaster management (GoHelp) and education (GoTeach) and support employee volunteerism (Global Volunteer Day, Living Responsibility Fund)
Find out more about our CR activities
GoHelp With our GoHelp program we provide the United Nations with access to our core competency in logistics and our global network.
Find out more
GoGreen The primary focus of our GoGreen program is to minimize our business operations’ greatest impact on the environment - CO2 emissions.
Find out more
GoTeach In our GoTeach program we aim at turning society’s attention to improve future opportunities of young people.
Find out more
