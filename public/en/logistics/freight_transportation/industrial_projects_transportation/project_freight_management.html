Project Freight Management

With extensive project logistics experience, DHL proudly provides integrated turnkey forwarding services to meet the demands of any industry sector solution in any part of the world.

Project Freight Management

When your material weighs several hundred thousand tons, cannot be fitted into standard freight equipment such as trucks, ocean freight containers, aircraft or ships, and needs to be mobilized from vendors all over the world to reach final construction sites in remote locations, you can count on the expertise of DHL Industrial Projects.
With end-to-end management and execution of all logistics aspects of complex global projects, we offer our clients perfect solutions in all areas ranging form cargo scheduling and materials management through tailored solutions for unique outsize cargoes and heavy lifts.

Our dedicated project teams at origin, destination, and all transit points in between (including client implants) ensure a successful project with complete visibility throughout the project from initiation to completion with live access and client-specific reporting.
