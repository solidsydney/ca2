Transborder Services

DHL is the link for shipping finished products or parts between the US, Canada and Mexico. With over 80 offices situated at the major airports throughout North America, DHL can provide customers with an unmatched selection of air, ground, customs and distribution.
DHL can help make shipping and distributing products into Canada as easy as shipping within the US. DHL assists US exporters in achieving Non-Resident Importer Status in Canada, thus allowing exporters to ship consolidated customer orders to themselves c/o DHL Global Forwarding(Canada), Inc.. DHL can then perform customs clearance and distribution across Canada.
Our south-of-the-border service is equally extensive. Between the United States and Mexico, DHL provides FTL/LTL, air and ocean options to the final destination, as well as customs brokerage, on-site and bilingual personnel, and warehousing/inventory control.

Whether your shipment is headed north or south, DHL offers a variety of transportation options to meet your specific needs.
