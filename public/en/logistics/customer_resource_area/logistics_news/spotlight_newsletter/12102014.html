The Spotlight Newsletter

 12/10/2014

DHL Global Forwarding announces upcoming office and warehouse move in Chicago

We are pleased to inform you DHL Global Forwarding will be officially moving to its new location in Chicago, Illinois, effective Monday, January 5, 2015. This new state-of-the-art facility will be DHL Global Forwarding’s largest facility in the country. The new building and warehouse will be located at the Chicago O’Hare International Airport’s cargo zone at:11601 W. Touhy Avenue, Building 895 Chicago, Illinois 60666. Please click here to view maps.

We are providing maps and specific directions with this correspondence as our new facility began as a parcel of vacant O’Hare airport property. As such, the official address has not been recognized on Internet based mapping programs to date. Please follow the details on the attached document and this will direct all parties to our facility.

The inauguration of this 500,000 square-foot office and warehouse space is a significant period in our history that represents our commitment and support to helping you grow your business. As part of this commitment, we want to keep you informed of minor changes that could possibly impact you.

DHL Global Forwarding will remain fully operational at its current location in Franklin Park until Sunday, January 4, with no expected disturbances.

We recommend, if applicable, that you update all billing with the new address listed above, and share it with your vendors doing direct drop shipments, and the Department of State for all import and export licensing. You should also adjust any internal standard operating procedures, if necessary.

Please note, for customs clearance transactions, DHL Global Forwarding will continue using its FIRMS code I342 at its new location.

Lastly, beginning Monday, January 5, all customers shipping directly or through third party trucking companies will be required to check-in all shipments with the Dispatch office located on the South side of the new facility between doors 22S and 23S. DHL Global Forwarding will continue to offer trucking drop-off and operations 24 hours/7 days per week at this new location.

Thank you for your ongoing support and for trusting in DHL Global Forwarding for your logistics needs. We appreciate your patience as we embark on this new journey. If you have any questions, please contact your DHL Global Forwarding sales representative.
